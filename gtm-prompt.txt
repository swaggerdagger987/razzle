### PHASE 3: CRITIQUE (find every gap)

After writing the report, STOP and critique your own work. For EVERY section ask:

1. **Evidence check**: Did I cite actual data or did I make this up? Every claim needs a source — a Reddit post, a web search result, a competitor's pricing page, or a calculation. Flag any claim without evidence as [NEEDS DATA].

2. **Specificity check**: Did I say "many dynasty managers" or did I say "r/DynastyFF has 180k subscribers with ~500 daily active posters"? Vague = useless. Flag vague claims as [TOO VAGUE].

3. **Honesty check**: Am I being optimistic because I want Razzle to succeed, or am I reporting what the data actually says? If the data says the market is smaller than we hoped, say that. Flag sugar-coating as [REALITY CHECK].

4. **Actionability check**: Can the founder read this section and know EXACTLY what to do Monday morning? If not, it's not specific enough. Flag hand-wavy advice as [NOT ACTIONABLE].

5. **Contradiction check**: Does this section contradict another section? Does the pricing recommendation match the market sizing? Does the channel strategy match the target persona?

Write all flags into a CRITIQUE section at the bottom of the report.

### PHASE 4: FILL GAPS (research what's missing)

For every [NEEDS DATA], [TOO VAGUE], [REALITY CHECK], and [NOT ACTIONABLE] flag:

1. **Generate a specific research question** — not "learn more about pricing" but "what does Dynasty Nerds charge for their dynasty package and what's included?"

2. **Research it** — web search, Reddit search, or competitor site browse. Use the tools available to you.

3. **Integrate the finding** into the report section, replacing the flag with real data.

4. **If you can't find the answer**, say "UNKNOWN — could not find data on [X]. Recommendation: [how to find out]." Don't guess.

### PHASE 5: SECOND CRITIQUE

Run the critique again on the updated report. Are the flags resolved? Are there NEW gaps visible now that the first gaps are filled? This is the autoresearch loop — each pass exposes deeper questions.

If more than 5 flags remain after Phase 4, run Phases 4-5 one more time (max 3 total critique passes per cycle).
