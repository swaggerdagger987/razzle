## Pricing page headline "Pick Your Playbook" is generic and doesn't communicate the core value prop

**PRIORITY: P2** | **Type: copy**
**Page**: pricing.html
**Found by**: Razzle (CEO Review)

"Pick Your Playbook" is a sports metaphor that could be on any fantasy football site. It doesn't communicate Razzle's unique positioning: the Screener is forever free, the intelligence is what you pay for.

**BEFORE** (what it is now):
- Headline: "Pick Your Playbook"
- Subheadline: "The Screener is forever free. Pro and Elite unlock the intelligence."

**AFTER** (what it should be):
- Headline: "The Screener is forever free."
- Subheadline: "No account. No catch. No trial that expires. Pro and Elite add the intelligence layer."

The brand line IS the pricing headline. Don't invent a new one. Let the thing that makes Razzle different be the first thing people read on the pricing page.

**WHY**: The pricing page headline should reinforce the most differentiated aspect of the business model -- genuine forever-free tier. "Pick Your Playbook" wastes the most valuable real estate on the pricing page on a generic metaphor.

### Task 1: Update pricing page headline to lead with "forever free"
**Accept when**: The pricing page headline is "The Screener is forever free." and the subheadline clarifies what Pro/Elite add.
