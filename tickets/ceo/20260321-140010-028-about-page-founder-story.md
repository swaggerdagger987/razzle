# Ticket 028 — About Page: Founder Story

**ID**: 20260321-140010-028
**Page**: About
**Type**: structural
**Severity**: P1
**Created**: 2026-03-21

## Problem

The About page has attribution, privacy info, and a charming headline ("the fine print, if you're into that sort of thing"). But it has no founder story, no "why I built this," no team info.

For a $80-150/yr product, users want to know who's behind it. A founder story builds trust and creates emotional connection — especially in fantasy football where community and personality matter.

## BEFORE

About page: data attribution, privacy policy, contact email. No story. No face behind the product.

## AFTER

About page adds a "Why Razzle" section above the fine print:

- **Who**: "Built by one person with Claude Code as a force multiplier." (per north star)
- **Why**: "I got tired of copy-pasting my roster into ChatGPT every week. So I built a tool that already knows my league."
- **The promise**: "The Screener is forever free. No catch. No bait-and-switch. If Razzle ever charges for the Screener, I'll eat my own tiger tail."
- **The vibe**: 3-4 sentences in Razzle's voice — direct, slightly self-deprecating, no corporate speak
- **Link to Twitter**: @razzle_lol

Keep the existing attribution and privacy sections. Add the story above them.

## Why This Matters

Reddit's r/DynastyFF community respects builders who are transparent about their projects. "I built this" posts with genuine backstory get 10x the engagement of faceless product launches. The About page is where that authenticity lives.

## Accept When

About page has a "Why Razzle" section with founder story in Razzle's voice. Twitter link included. Existing content preserved below.
