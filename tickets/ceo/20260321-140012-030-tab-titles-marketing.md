# Ticket 030 — Browser Tab Titles for Open-Tab Marketing

**ID**: 20260321-140012-030
**Page**: Global
**Type**: copy
**Severity**: P2
**Created**: 2026-03-21

## Problem

Browser tab titles are functional but not compelling:
- "Screener — Razzle" (most common)
- "Bureau of Intelligence — Razzle"
- "Situation Room — Razzle"
- "Pricing — Razzle"

When a user has 15 tabs open, "Screener — Razzle" doesn't stand out. It doesn't remind them why they opened this tab.

## BEFORE

Tab titles: "{Page Name} — Razzle" — descriptive but forgettable.

## AFTER

Tab titles that sell the value:
- Home: "Razzle — Free Fantasy Football Research Lab"
- Lab: "Razzle Lab — 100+ Stats, Custom Formulas, Forever Free"
- Bureau: "Your League Intel — Razzle"
- Situation Room: "AI Agents for Your League — Razzle"
- Pricing: "Razzle — The Screener Is Free. The Intelligence Is What You Pay For."
- Individual panels (when loaded): "Dynasty Rankings — Razzle Lab"

The tab title is micro-copy that works 24/7 in the browser tab bar. It's the last thing a user sees before deciding whether to come back to this tab or close it.

## Accept When

All page titles updated per the above. Panel-specific titles load dynamically when a Lab panel is selected.
