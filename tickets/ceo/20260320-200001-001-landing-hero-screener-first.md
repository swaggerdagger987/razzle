## The landing page hero leads with AI comparison instead of the Screener

**PRIORITY: P0** | **Type: structural**
**Page**: index.html
**Found by**: Razzle (CEO Review)

The hero headline is "ChatGPT doesn't know your league. Razzle does." This positions Razzle as an AI tool competing with ChatGPT. But the north star is explicit: the Screener is the front door. The Screener is the growth engine. Every screenshot is a billboard.

A stranger from r/DynastyFF clicking in doesn't care about ChatGPT. They care about finding breakout candidates, evaluating trades, and discovering sleepers. The hero should make the Screener sound irresistible, not explain why Razzle is better than ChatGPT.

The ChatGPT comparison is a great line -- but it belongs in the Bureau/Situation Room sections lower on the page, where league context is the sell. The hero should sell the free tool.

**BEFORE** (what it is now):
- Hero headline: "ChatGPT doesn't know your league. Razzle does."
- Subheadline: "The Screener is forever free. The intelligence is what you pay for."
- CTAs: "Open the Screener" and "Connect your league" side by side

**AFTER** (what it should be):
- Hero headline: "The fantasy football research lab. Forever free."
- Subheadline: "100+ stat columns. Custom formulas. Shareable views. No account required. No catch."
- Primary CTA (large, orange): "Open the Screener"
- Secondary CTA (smaller, outlined): "See what's inside" (scrolls to feature breakdown)
- Move "ChatGPT doesn't know your league" to the Bureau section lower on the page where it actually makes sense contextually

**WHY**: The Screener is the top of the funnel. Every user who converts to paid started by using the free Screener. The hero should sell the free tool with zero friction, not position against ChatGPT which requires the user to already know what AI fantasy tools are. This directly impacts the first 3 seconds of every new visitor's experience -- the highest leverage moment in the entire conversion funnel.

### Task 1: Rewrite hero section to lead with the Screener value prop
**Accept when**: The hero headline communicates "free research lab" and the primary CTA goes to the Screener. The ChatGPT line appears in the Bureau section instead.
