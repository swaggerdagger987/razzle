# Ticket 026 — Nav Mega-Menu for Panel Discovery

**ID**: 20260321-140008-026
**Page**: Global nav
**Type**: structural
**Severity**: P1
**Created**: 2026-03-21

## Problem

The main nav has 5 links: Home, Screener, Bureau, Situation Room, Pricing. The footer has 5 columns with 40+ organized links (Dynasty, Weekly, Analytics, Tools, Razzle). The footer accidentally provides better navigation than the nav.

Users who want to find "aging curves" or "trade values" have two options: scroll to the footer, or open the Lab and search the 68-panel sidebar. Neither is discoverable from the nav.

Review #1 ticket 004 identified that "Bureau" and "Situation Room" are insider jargon. This ticket supersedes 004 by replacing the flat nav with a mega-menu that solves both the jargon problem AND the discovery problem.

## BEFORE

Nav: Home | Screener | Bureau | Situation Room | Pricing — five flat links, two of which are jargon.

## AFTER

Nav: Home | **The Lab** (dropdown) | **Your League** | **AI Agents** | Pricing

**"The Lab" dropdown** (mega-menu on hover/click):
- **Quick access**: Screener, Dashboard, Dynasty Rankings, Trade Values
- **Dynasty**: Rankings, Tiers, Trade Values, Aging Curves, Buy/Sell, VORP
- **Weekly**: Heatmap, Matchups, Leaders, Waivers, Stocks
- **Analytics**: Efficiency, Usage, Air Yards, Red Zone, Breakouts
- **Tools**: Compare, Explorer, Cheat Sheet, Roster Builder
- **All 68 tools →** (links to Lab with full sidebar)

"Your League" replaces "Bureau" — plain English.
"AI Agents" replaces "Situation Room" — descriptive, not jargon.

Mobile: mega-menu becomes an accordion.

## Why This Matters

The nav is the site's primary navigation. Every page has it. Making the Lab's 68 tools discoverable from the nav means users can jump directly to any tool from any page, without opening the Lab sidebar first.

## Accept When

"The Lab" nav item opens a categorized dropdown showing popular panels by category. "Bureau" and "Situation Room" are renamed to plain English. Mobile accordion works.
