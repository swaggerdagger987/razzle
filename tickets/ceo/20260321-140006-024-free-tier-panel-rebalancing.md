# Ticket 024 — Free Tier Panel Rebalancing

**ID**: 20260321-140006-024
**Page**: Lab
**Type**: strategic
**Severity**: P1
**Created**: 2026-03-21

## Problem

Current free panels (10): Screener, Dynasty Rankings, Tiers, Trade Values, Cheat Sheet, Breakouts, Weekly Heatmap, Big Board, Dashboard, Stat Leaders.

Dynasty Rankings, Trade Values, and Breakouts are the most compelling panels — the ones that create "I need more" moments. Giving them away for free eliminates the upgrade trigger. A free user who already has Dynasty Rankings, Trade Values, AND Breakouts has little incentive to pay $80/yr for "more of the same."

The free tier should be generous enough to hook, not generous enough to satisfy.

## BEFORE

10 free panels including the 3 most conversion-driving panels (Dynasty Rankings, Trade Values, Breakouts). Free users get the best stuff. Pro upgrade feels incremental.

## AFTER

5 free panels: **Screener, Dashboard, Weekly Heatmap, Big Board, Stat Leaders.**

Move to Pro (with blurred preview + upgrade CTA):
- **Dynasty Rankings** — "See where your players rank in dynasty. Upgrade to Pro."
- **Trade Values** — "Find your players' trade value. Upgrade to Pro."
- **Breakouts** — "3 breakout candidates match your roster. Upgrade to Pro."
- **Cheat Sheet** — "Draft day prep starts here. Upgrade to Pro."
- **Tiers** — "See your players' tier placement. Upgrade to Pro."

Each locked panel shows:
1. Blurred preview of real data (visible enough to see it's valuable, blurred enough to be unusable)
2. Panel name and one-line value prop
3. One-click "Unlock with Pro" CTA
4. "7-day free trial, no credit card" subtitle

## Why This Matters

The 5 remaining free panels (Screener, Dashboard, Weekly Heatmap, Big Board, Stat Leaders) are still genuinely impressive. The Screener alone is best-in-class. But now the FOMO panels (rankings, values, breakouts) are behind the paywall where they drive conversion.

## Accept When

Free users see 5 unlocked panels and 63 locked panels with blurred previews. Dynasty Rankings, Trade Values, and Breakouts show compelling previews that drive upgrade clicks.
