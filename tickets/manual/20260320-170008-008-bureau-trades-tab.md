## Build the Bureau Trades tab with trade finder suggestions

**PRIORITY: P1** | **Type: structural**
**Page**: league-intel.html
**Design doc**: docs/bureau-design.md

The Trades tab is PRO-gated and surfaces actionable trade suggestions based on roster needs and rival behavioral profiles. It cross-references the user's Self-Scout weakness areas with rival managers who have surplus at those positions, filtered by behavioral tag (prioritize PANIC SELLER and AGGRESSIVE managers who are more likely to deal). Each suggestion shows the target player, their manager's behavioral tag, and a Bones-voiced rationale.

### Task 1: Implement Trades tab with trade finder
**Accept when**: Pro users see 3-5 trade suggestions ranked by feasibility. Each suggestion shows: target player name, position, PPG, owning manager name, manager behavioral tag, and a one-line Bones rationale. Suggestions are generated by matching user's weakest positions against rivals with depth at those positions. Free users see a blurred preview with upgrade CTA.
