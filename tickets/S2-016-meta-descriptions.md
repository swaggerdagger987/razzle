# S2-016: Some standalone pages have similar meta descriptions

## Finding

The deep audit says several pages have similar meta descriptions reducing SEO value.

## Root Cause Investigation

**Status: Already properly implemented.**

Verified 6 standalone pages — all have unique, keyword-rich meta descriptions:

- **efficiency.html:20** — "Fantasy efficiency rankings... Points per opportunity, yards per touch, catch rate, and A+ to F efficiency grades."
- **consistency.html:20** — "Fantasy consistency rankings... coefficient of variation, with floor/ceiling percentile ranges."
- **schedule.html:20** — "Strength of schedule analysis... who overcame tough matchups and who padded stats against weak defenses."
- **vorp.html:20** — "Value Over Replacement Player rankings... replacement-level thresholds (QB12, RB24, WR36, TE12)."
- **airyards.html:20** — "Air yards dashboard... aDOT, RACR, WOPR, air yard share, and regression indicators."
- **breakouts.html:20** — "Fantasy breakout candidate finder... opportunity volume outpaces their production."

All descriptions are contextual, specific, and include relevant keywords. No duplicates.

## Conclusion

No action needed. Meta descriptions are already unique per page.
