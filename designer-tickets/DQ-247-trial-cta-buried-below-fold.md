# 7-day trial CTA buried below comparison table

## What's wrong
Razzle offers a 7-day free trial — a strong conversion lever. But the trial mention is buried in the FAQ section below a 25-row comparison table. A visitor who scrolls past the plan cards and doesn't immediately click "Start Pro" will hit the comparison table, get overwhelmed, and bounce before ever seeing "try it free for 7 days."

## Why it matters
The trial is the answer to "what if I don't like it?" — the #1 objection at the pricing page. It should be visible within the first viewport, ideally on or near the Pro CTA button.

## Fix
1. Add trial mention directly on the Pro plan CTA: "Start 7-day free trial" instead of generic "Get Pro"
2. Add a small line under the CTA: "No charge for 7 days. Cancel anytime."
3. Optionally add a trial banner between plan cards and comparison table: "Not sure? Try Pro free for 7 days — no credit card required" (if that's accurate)
