Questions we keep getting

What's the difference between Pro and Elite? ▼
Same analytical tools. Elite adds server-side AI agents (no API key needed, unlimited queries) and priority support. Pro: you bring your own API key from OpenRouter (60 seconds, costs $1-3/month in usage). Think of Pro as the DIY option and Elite as the easy button.
Is there a free trial? ▼
Yes. Every new account gets 7 days of full Pro access automatically. No credit card required. Just sign up and you're in. After 7 days, you can subscribe to keep Pro or continue using the Screener for free.
Can I cancel anytime? ▼
Yes. Cancel from your account at any time. You keep access through the end of your billing period. No cancellation fees, no hassle.
Do I need a Sleeper account? ▼
No. The Screener works without Sleeper. But connecting Sleeper unlocks league-contextualized AI — agents analyze YOUR roster, YOUR rivals, YOUR league's scoring settings. That's where the real value is.
What happens when my trial ends? ▼
You keep the Screener and all your saved work — formulas, views, watchlist. Nothing is lost. The Pro panels and Situation Room lock until you subscribe. Pick it back up any time.
Which API keys work with Pro? ▼
OpenRouter (recommended — gives access to GPT-4o, Claude, Gemini, and more), OpenAI, or Anthropic. Set your key in the Situation Room settings. Your key is encrypted and never logged.
What formats do the agents support? ▼
All of them. Redraft, dynasty, keeper, best ball, superflex, 2QB, IDP. The agents detect your league format from Sleeper settings and adapt their analysis automatically. A redraft question gets a different answer than a dynasty question.
Do you offer refunds? ▼
If you're not happy within the first 14 days, email swaggerdagger987@gmail.com and we'll sort it out. No questions asked.
Is the Screener really free? ▼
Yes. 100+ stat columns, 70+ analytical panels, custom formulas, radar charts, scatter plots, prospect screener, shareable URLs, image export. All forever free. No account required. The Screener is the product — Pro and Elite unlock the intelligence on top.
Is my API key safe? ▼
Your key is stored in your browser only — we never see it. Browser extensions on the page could technically read it, so we recommend creating a dedicated key with a monthly spending cap on OpenRouter. That way you're covered even in the worst case.
