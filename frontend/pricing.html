Frequently Asked Questions

What is BYOK (Bring Your Own Key)?
Pro users bring their own API key from OpenRouter, OpenAI, or Anthropic. You pay the LLM provider directly for usage (usually pennies per query). Elite users don't need a key — we cover all AI costs.
Is there a free trial?
Yes. Every paid plan includes a 7-day free trial. Your card is charged after the trial ends. Cancel anytime before that and you won't be charged.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel from your account at any time. You keep access through the end of your billing period. No cancellation fees, no hassle.
Do I need a Sleeper account?
No. The Lab works without Sleeper. But connecting Sleeper unlocks league-contextualized AI — agents analyze YOUR roster, YOUR rivals, YOUR league's scoring settings. That's where the real value is.
What happens when my trial ends?
Your plan converts to a paid subscription at the rate you chose (monthly or yearly). If you cancel during the trial, you drop back to the free tier. Your formulas and saved views stay — nothing is lost.
Which API keys work with Pro?
OpenRouter (recommended — gives access to GPT-4o, Claude, Gemini, and more), OpenAI, or Anthropic. Set your key in the Situation Room settings. Your key is encrypted and never logged.
What formats do the agents support?
All of them. Redraft, dynasty, keeper, best ball, superflex, 2QB, IDP. The agents detect your league format from Sleeper settings and adapt their analysis automatically. A redraft question gets a different answer than a dynasty question.
Is The Lab really free forever?
Yes. 100+ stat columns, 60+ analytical panels, custom formulas, radar charts, scatter plots, prospect screener, shareable URLs, image export. All free. No account required. The Lab is the product — we just charge for the AI agents on top.
