FAQ
▶ What does it cost?
Most queries cost $0.003-$0.01. A $5 budget covers ~500-1,000 queries.
▶ Which model should I use?
openrouter/auto (default) works great. For more detailed analysis, try anthropic/claude-3.5-haiku.
▶ What if my key stops working?
Check your OpenRouter balance at openrouter.ai/credits.
▶ Is my key safe?
Your key is stored in your browser session only — it's never sent to Razzle's servers. Set a spending limit for extra safety.
